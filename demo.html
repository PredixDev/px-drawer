px-drawer

The px-drawer component lets you store content outside the viewport.

There are 3 types of drawer.

  1. temporary: Recommended for: Tablet Required for:Mobile
  2. persistent: Persistent navigation drawers are acceptable for all sizes larger than mobile
  3. mini: Recommended for Apps that need quick selection access alongside content
